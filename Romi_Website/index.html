ROMI Home is designed for residential use and smart home integration. ROMI Play is specifically designed for child interaction and educational purposes. All models require proper setup, calibration, and regular maintenance. Users must be 18 years or older to operate ROMI Care and ROMI Home models. ROMI Play requires adult supervision for children under 12. The emotional recognition and AI interaction features are intended to enhance user experience and should not replace professional medical, psychological, or educational services.
